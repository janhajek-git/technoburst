ROG Strix Z590-E Motherboard

Attention gamers! If you’re looking for a better-performing motherboard to fire up your system, this is what you’ll need. 

The ROG Strix Z590-E is a motherboard specially designed for gamers. It is priced at $374 and comes with a set of instructions, an extra fan (40mm), a set of screws and plastic cords to help you fix everything in place, and so much more!

Let’s look at their details and specifications in-depth, shall we?

It is a motherboard that has been specially designed for gamers and gaming purposes alone. Some of their standout features include the following.

First Class AI features

This motherboard has a gaming processor that uses AI Technology for the purpose of noise cancelation, system cooling, and system over-clocking and networking.

That helps you to simplify the process of setup and also provides you with a better experience.

Extensive USB slot options

It has a number of USB slots, including 4 slots with heat-dissipating sinks for M2 and USB slots for the 3.2 gen and SATA of type–C. 

Compatibility

This motherboard is compatible with both of Intel’s 11th and 10th generation processors. It is also compatible with the latest WiFi type – the 6E.

Suitable Power Options

They come with power points of 14+2 that come along with a ProCool II power adapter. It also comes with reliable capacitors made of superior and durable alloy metal to help run these processors. 

Suitable Designs To Handle Heat

A high-performing motherboard tends to release a lot of heat while functioning. Hence this has been designed in such a way that it can remove the heat safely without overworking itself.

It has a heat-dissipating sink made of aluminum, a heat pipe, 4 built-in sinks to release heat, and other similar outlets.

Efficient Networking Properties

Gamers usually play in teams that require a lot of communicating. That is why this motherboard is designed to be compatible with the latest WiFi. (WiFi 6E or 802.11ax).

Along with the LANGuard of ASUS, it is also designed to support Ethernet of 2.5GB.

High Connectivity For The Best Gaming Experience

Ask any gamer what the most important part of the experience is, and they’ll tell you it’s the graphics. Visuals play a huge role and contribute a lot to the overall experience. 

That is why this motherboard is designed to support the latest version of HDMI (Version 2.0). It also has a port for display and visuals, 4 slots with heat-dissipating sinks for M2, and USB slots for the 3.2 gen and SATA of type–C. 

Design That Is User Friendly

Most of these setups are fairly easy to handle if you have basic knowledge of how laptops and PCs function. 

They come with one FlexKey, one FlashBack (by BIOS), and other programs that help you set up this system easier. 

Option Of Personalization

This particular motherboard allows the user to make changes to suit their personality. RGB changes can be made in the lighting and headers.  

Complete Audio

The second most important aspect to an enjoyable gaming experience is the audio. Audio helps the experience more realistic, which makes it more enjoyable. 

This motherboard includes the amplifier from Savitech (SV3H712) along with DTS’s Sound Unbound program with Sonic Studio III.

Overall Powerful Software

This motherboard also comes with professional software like the dashboard from UEI BIOS, combined with MemTest86.

MSI Z590 PRO Motherboard

This motherboard from MSI has definitely got to be one of the more affordable motherboards in the market today. For a price of $211 it has quite a lot of good features to it.

However, it does have a few negatives to it. So, the question here is, do the pros outweigh the cons? It surely does!

Let’s take a quick look at their top features.

Compatible Motherboard

This motherboard is specially designed to work with Intel’s 11 Generation Core Processor. But if you can’t afford it at the moment, don’t worry! The MSI motherboard is suitable for use with Intel’s 10 Generation Core Processor as well.

Accommodates A Variety Of USB Ports 

This motherboard allows you to plug in as many devices as you want, thanks to the plentiful number of options that MSI has provided. 

It has 1 type–C port, 1 type–C internal connector, and 1 type-A port, all on the motherboard’s back panel. It also has 2 type-A ports that you can find on the back panel, and 4 other ports can be found on the motherboard itself.

It has a 2.0 version of the type–A port located on the back panel, and 4 USB ports (version 2.0) can be used through internal USBs.

Expansive Memory And Storage Options

They can really take in a lot of materials because of the 4 DDR4 memory slots. In total, they can support up to 128GB of data. When used with Intel’s 11th Gen processor, they can easily run about 5333 MHz and maybe even more!

Captivating Visual And Sound Effects

To provide you with high-quality visuals with the highest resolution of up to 4K 60Hz, the MSI motherboard has preinstalled HDMI software (version 2.0) along with an HDR port. It also has a DisplayPort that also offers you the same resolution.

A Realtek software (Model ALC897 Codec with 7.1-Channel High Definition Audio) has also been preinstalled in the motherboard to give you a full surround sound experience.

It will almost feel like you’re living the PC experience with these visual and audio software in real-time!

Network And Data Connectivity

It also provides you the feature of connecting through Bluetooth and WiFi. The wireless unit is already installed in the motherboard, so there’s no need for you to purchase and manually install it individually.

It is also compatible with Bluetooth, and FIPS and FISMA have approved it.

ASUS Prime H510M-E Motherboard

In general, the Prime series of Asus is designed to handle a student’s workload or someone who is working from home. It was not originally created for the purpose of gaming, but surprisingly, the motherboard performs well under that situation as well!

It is also very affordable. But is it just as good as the expensive ones? Let’s find out.

Powered By The Latest

This motherboard can be powered by Intel’s latest 11th Gen processor. Although it is designed to fit the latest model, it can also work well with Intel’s 10th Gen processor.

It also has the H510 chipset for better performance.

Storage And Memory Functions

For storing data and information, this motherboard uses the 2DDR4 program (which is dual channel). Hence, it can store data up to 64GB. 

It is also compatible with Intel’s XMP (stands for Extreme Memory profile). That adds to the overall capacity and increases the total storage space.

Outstanding Graphics And Audio Quality

In terms of graphics and visuals, this motherboard has 1 DisplayPort (Version 1.4), 1 D-Sub with HDMI features.

If you are using the motherboard with Intel’s 11th Gen processor, your HDMI 2.0 will support a maximum resolution of 4K at 30Hz. 

Keep in mind that the exact specifications for graphics will differ based on the types of CPU.

Now, let’s look at audio.

This motherboard is preinstalled RealTek Surround Sound High Definition (Version 7.1) for audio. This software can detect when an audio jack has been plugged or unplugged from the system. 

It can also stream multiple audios simultaneously and hold up to 192kHz or 24-Bit playback. 

It also comes with features of audio shielding and uses quality Japanese capacitors just for audio. It also comes with audio PCB layers. 

With these combined features, you can experience fantastic visuals with full surround sound. 

Cooling System

When you work on a PC or a laptop, it tends to heat up. That is normal as long as the heat finds a way out. That is why cooling fans are installed inside. 

This processor has 1 CPU Fan Header with 4 pins and 2 Chassis Fan Headers with 4 pins. It’ll help keep the system cool even as you work overtime. 

Power Functions

It has 1 central power connector with 24-pin and 1 power connector with 8-pin and 12V. 

Storage And USB Features

They consist of 1 M.2 slot (Key M) and 4 SATA 6Gb/s ports to help with internal storage. When it comes to USB, they have 1 USB (Version 3.2 Gen) with 1 header that carries another extra 2 USB (Version 3.2 Gen 1) ports. 

Aside from this, they also have 2 USB (Version 2.0) headers that carry another 4 USB (Version 2.0) ports.

It can give any PC/Laptop an ample amount of space for storing their data. 

Other than the usual features that come with almost all motherboards, Asus adds extra features that further improve the board.

ASUS TUF B560M-PLUS 

Motherboard

Yet another motherboard specially designed for gamers but not necessarily limited to pro gaming purposes. 

It is a very high-speed motherboard that can also be used for people who are working from home or college students completing their assignments!

Just like the other gaming motherboard, this also has a very unique set of features. Let’s take a detailed look at them to understand better.

Processing System

These motherboards are specifically designed to be powered by Intel’s 11th Gen processor. It is the latest model and has a lot to offer for your PC!

Advanced Power Solutions

They have 8+1 different power levels, a PCB of 6 different layers, a ProCool socket, and strong capacitors for uniform and stable delivery of power at all times. These power solutions keep your PC/Laptop running for a long time without any lag so you can have an uninterrupted workflow.

Detailed Cooling System

As mentioned earlier, this motherboard has around 4 power solutions to help you stay focused on your work. However, this might start to cause some heat inside the system.

That is normal and it happens quite often. But the heat does need a way to leave the system in order to keep functioning normally. 

And that’s precisely why this motherboard also has a VRM and PCH heat-dissipating sink, a versatile M.2 heat-dissipating sink, and fan headers. They also offer the software: Fan Xpert 2+, and all these combined will keep your system sunning even when they are on for a long time. 

Immense Connectivity

Gamers usually don’t play by themselves. They play in groups, and they all stay in communication while doing so. In order for a smooth communication process, the PC needs to have software like PCIe 4.0, Realtek 2.5GB Ethernet, WiFi 6, rear USB 3.2 Gen 2, and more.

It will keep the conversation and the game going with absolute ease.

Online Gaming Softwares

This motherboard is specially built and designed for gaming purposes. And what good is a gaming motherboard without relevant software?

This model comes with these software programs – Intel WiFi 6, Realtek 2.5 GB Ethernet, TUF LANGuard, and Turbo LAN technology.

These programs help in keeping your gaming session alive and interactive. 

Enhanced Audio Features

The audio of a game is one of the features that make the gaming experience fun. This motherboard helps in reducing any background noise from the mic set and a crisp audio output. And what do you get? Undisturbed voice transmission between you and your teammates.
