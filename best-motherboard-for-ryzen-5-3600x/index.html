Best Value Motherboard For Ryzen 5 3600x – MSI B450M Mortar MAX

MSI produces some of the best motherboards that we often use in our build. MSI is one of the best and well-known brands that produce quality products, including motherboards. We want to introduce to you the MSI B450M Mortar MAX motherboard. It is a MicroATX motherboard design for providing great performance.

The MSI B450M Mortar MAX motherboard supports 3rd generation processors, including APUs that are still running on a Vega graphics card. It has 4 DIMM slots with a maximum capacity of 128GB and supports clock speed up to 4133Mhz when overclocked, which helps you to achieve fast performance in gaming and productivity workload. Likewise, it accompanies a dual M.2 slot, including Turbo M.2 utilizing Gen3 x4 that can process data transfer speed up to 32 GB/s.

Also, the best thing about this motherboard is that it comes with a much better thermal cooling performance to maintain the low CPU temperature, which is ideal for the Ryzen 5 3600x since the CPU is packed with multiple cores. Along with a fully controllable fan header that allows you to manage the speed and temperature for all other connected fans to maintain a silent but cool system.

The MSI B450M Mortar MAX is an amazing motherboard, and we highly recommend this to partner with your Ryzen 5 3600x. If you want to achieve decent overclocking, this motherboard is the best choice for you. We have seen a lot of positive reviews regarding the performance of this motherboard. We dont recommend you to buy super cheap motherboards since the vast majority of them have poor thermal cooling performance and lack of ability in overclocking. The MSI B450M Mortar MAX will work perfectly fine with your Ryzen 5 3600x CPU.

There is also a B450 Tomahawk, an option if you prefer an ATX form factor. It delivers almost the same performance in terms of VRMs and overclocking. The only difference is that the B450 Tomahawk supports only a single M.2 and has a maximum memory capacity of 64GB. However, if you are okay with that, the B450 Tomahawk is a fine choice for you, you just need to make sure to update the BIOS.

Cheapest Motherboard For Ryzen 5 3600x – MSI B450 Gaming Plus Max

This is another entry for the MSI motherboards. The MSI B450 Gaming Plus Max, it is an ATX motherboard and the cheapest motherboard that we recommend to partner with your Ryzen 5 3600x. This motherboard also comes with a good VRM just like the MSI B450M Mortar MAX that I mentioned above. This motherboard will still provide great performance on your build. If you want to achieve decent overclocking but dont want to spend much money on the motherboard, we highly recommend the MSI B450 Gaming Plus Max motherboard.

This motherboard comes with 6 SATA ports but only has a single M.2, which is the Ultra M.2 that processes data speed up to 32GB/s. It has 4 DIMM slots with a maximum capacity of 128GB and can support clock speed up to 4133Mhz when overclocked. The only downside with this motherboard is that it only has a single M.2. However, if you are planning to utilize only a single M.2, this motherboard is a good choice for you.

Overall, this MSI B450 Gaming Plus Max motherboard will work perfectly fine with your Ryzen 5 3600x or other 3rd generation CPUs. Aside from that, its really difficult to fault this board for the price. If you really want to minimize your spending plan but still want to achieve great performance, this motherboard is highly recommended for you.

Best ITX Motherboard For Ryzen 5 3600x – MSI B450I Gaming Plus AC

Of course, we have an option for people who are building a compact PC. I want to introduce the MSI B450I Gaming Plus AC motherboard, it is the best ITX motherboard that we recommend to partner with your Ryzen 5 3600x CPU; however, you need to make sure that you update the BIOS. It comes with good VRMs so you can still perform light overclocking. And Ultra M.2, which is located at the back of this motherboard. Also, it comes with a WIFI, which is a great advantage since most motherboards that are mentioned in this guide don’t have the WIFI feature.

However, we will be still expecting that there are some cons to this type of motherboard since it is an ITX form factor. This ITX motherboard comes only with 2 available DIMM slots, which is quite insufficient for most of us; however, if you only plan to occupy only 2 slots, this motherboard is a great choice for you. Also, this motherboard doesn’t support multi GPU setup since it only has a single full-length PCIe slot. We highly advise you to check first your existing components before buying this motherboard to make sure that this will support your existing build.

Overall, this MSI B450I Gaming Plus AC ITX motherboard will still deliver great performance on your build. If you are planning to build a compact PC and want to minimize the space, this motherboard is a great choice for you. This board will work perfectly fine with your Ryzen 5 3600x CPU.
