Curve White Gaming Monitor – Acer ED242QR

If you prefer curved ones, there is no reason not to buy the Acer ED242QR Gaming Monitor. Acer produces some of the best monitors that we often use in gaming and different activities. With its price point, this Acer ED242QR is definitely hard to beat for the features it has.

The Acer ED242QR is a 23.6-inch 1080p monitor with a refresh rate of 75hz. It also features a VA display so you can enjoy high-definition visuals along with an AMD FreeSync that resolves screen tearing issues for a much pleasing gaming experience. The wide viewpoint technology lets you share what’s on your screen with loved ones and friends, and remain genuine regardless of what edge you’re seeing from. Also, it comes with a response time of 4ms, which is quite good for achieving fast performance in gaming. It is ideal if you play fast-paced games like a first-person shooter.

Overall, this monitor will not just provide you the looks you want, but also the performance you need in both gaming and great visuals.

27-Inch White Gaming Monitor – Samsung Curved Monitor

As Samsung continuously produces monitors, they made some really nice gaming monitors. Despite the fact that their flagships are very costly, they still do have some more budget-friendly choices for gamers. In case you’re in the market for a white gaming monitor, the Samsung Curved 27″ model is one of the best curved white gaming monitors that we recommend for you to buy. 

It is a 27-inch monitor with a resolution of 1920×1080p. While this doesn’t bring about an excessive number of pixels per inch, it’s as yet a decent size for a screen you could undoubtedly use for performing various tasks, as well. It features a refresh rate of 60Hz, which isn’t excessively high, however, that is not the primary focal point of the Samsung monitor. 

What you do get, be that as it may, is a curve design with a 1800R curvature, which will bring about a pretty immersive experience while gaming. With its 1080p resolution, it will allow you to crank up the settings quite a bit, and you’ll truly get a lovely image of it.

Of course, we have an entry for the HP OMEN gaming series, the Pavilion 27xw. It comes with a futuristic pure white-looking appearance that surely maximizes the beauty of your gaming setup.
