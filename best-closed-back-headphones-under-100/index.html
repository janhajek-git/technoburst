How to Choose the Best Headphone?

To choose the ideal headphone you must first evaluate what the functionality of your phone will be and where you intend to use it. With these points in mind, check the specifications below and choose the ones that best suit your needs.

Bluetooth or Cable? Choose the Connection According to Your Daily Activities

The various wireless headphone models connect to devices via Bluetooth. These are great for those who like to listen to music while doing activities that require more mobility, such as running, walking on the street and weight training. With a Bluetooth headphone, you don’t have to worry about wires tangling and hindering your performance. But remember to check the battery life!

Cable headphones do not depend on the battery to operate. Therefore, they are great for those who need to use the equipment for hours at a time, in the office or playing games, for example. They use P2 or P3 connectors (with microphone), traditional connections on various devices. After all, it is worth remembering that some electronic devices do not have a Bluetooth connection.

Check for Headphone Noise Canceling

Some headphones have additional functions to minimize or block external noise. These models can even optimize the sound according to the environment where it is being used, allowing an appreciation totally focused on the audio that is transmitted on the headphone.

If you like to stay fully focused and have no problem investing a little more in the headphone, consider noise-canceling models. In general, these are premium headphones, which have advanced sound processing features, so they are more expensive!

Enhance Headphone Comfort, Prefer Adjustable Stems and Soft Cushions

As the headphone is used for long periods it needs to be as comfortable as possible to use. Evaluate customer comments on a particular product. Consider that the headphone must have adjustable rods, preferably coated, for you to adjust more easily on the head.

Also, evaluate the material of the cushions in the acoustic shells, some are made with foam that fits the ear. The lining of the cushion is also important, the fabric is fresher than leather or leather, however, it has less sound insulation. Assess whether you prefer more comfort or performance!

Media Control Is Much More Practical

In headphones with media control, you have more freedom and agility. With this function, you do not have to touch the device you are using all the time to play or rewind a song, adjust the volume or answer calls.

In wireless models, the media control is usually in the shell, with the options on / off, volume, pass/return, and answer call button. In wired headphones, however, it is common for the control to be located on the cable, next to the microphone (as much as you have). Prefer with that ease!
