Best Overall CPU Cooler For Ryzen 7 2700X – Noctua NH-D15

Noctua is a well-known company that produces a variety of different products, including CPU coolers. The Noctua NH-D15 CPU Cooler is a fantastic overall CPU cooler that many would find perfect for the Ryzen 7 2700x processor.

What makes this CPU cooler perfect for the Ryzen 7 2700x is the dual 140 mm fans. These fans are Noctua’s own NF-A15 fans. These fans offer a maximum RPM of 1,200 RPM when utilizing the Low Noise Adapter, but when not using the L.N.A., the maximum RPM is 1,500.

One fantastic thing about this CPU cooler is that it has enough space to install a third fan to the heatsink rear.

For noise, the CPU cooler only makes 19.2 dBA of noise when utilizing the Low Noise Adapter and makes 24.6 dBA noise when the L.N.A. isn’t installed into the system.

When combined with the massive heatsink that the NH-D15 CPU cooler, this heatsink has the heat pulled through six heat pipes, which are highly optimized to provide more surface area. This CPU cooler offers high RAM compatibility when only using a single fan in the Dual tower CPU cooler center.

This CPU cooler, the Noctua NH-D15 CPU cooler, is a perfect choice for the Ryzen 7 2700x, especially if the builder may be looking to upgrade to a different processor in the future.

Best Low Profile Cooler For Ryzen 7 2700X – Cooler Master MasterAir G200P RGB

While some PC builder’s setups are full-towers or Mid-towers that can offer compatibility for the larger CPU coolers, other PC cases can only offer up to a Mini-ITX motherboard. These cases usually require a low-profile cooler instead of the larger coolers that many PC enthusiasts use in their everyday system.

The Cooler Master MasterAir G200p RGB Low-Profile CPU air cooler, this low-profile cooler forgoes the common tower or dual tower design and has the fan resting on top of the heatsink instead of being attached to the side of the tower.

This CPU cooler measures just 39.4 mm in height, which means that the majority of Mini-ITX cases can offer support for this CPU cooler.

The Cooler Master MasterAir G200P utilizes a single 92 mm PWM fan with RGB lights in the fan hub. This fan offers a significantly wider speed range from 800 RPM to up to 2,600 RPM that produces a maximum airflow of 35.5 CFM. The fan at the maximum speed produces up to 28 dBA of sound.

This CPU cooler utilizes two heat pipes with a C-shaped design to offer an extremely compact design and maximize heat dissipation.

This CPU cooler should only be used with the Ryzen 7 2700x if the users have a Mini-ITX case instead of the standard mid-tower or full-tower PC case.

Quietest Air Cooler For Ryzen 7 2700X – be quiet! Dark Rock Pro 4

For users that want the quietest system, we highly recommend they be quiet! Dark Rock Pro 4. This CPU cooler offers a fantastic choice for the Ryzen 7 2700x processor because the maximum cooling capacity this CPU cooler offers is an astounding 250-watt TDP.

This astounding CPU cooler utilizes two virtually inaudible silent wings PWM fans; these fans utilize a six-pole fan motor that utilizes less power consumption and less vibration when compared to standard CPU fans.
These fans offer a virtually inaudible operation by producing just 16.4 dBA of sound and producing 50.5 CFM for the maximum airflow.

When utilizing two of these fans, the Dark Rock Pro 4 creates just 24.3 dBA of sound when at the maximum fan speed.

To cool the Ryzen 7 2700x, the Dark Rock Pro 4 utilizes a total of seven high-performance copper heat pipes, which is easily able to move the heat from the processor to the two dual tower heatsink.

While other CPU coolers utilize RGB lights, the Dark Rock Pro 4 has no RGB lighting and instead has a solid black design for both the fans and the black heatsink.

The immense cooling power offers a no-compromise in either silence and performance for overclocked systems; this CPU cooler achieves low temperatures even during peak CPU performance periods.

Best AIO Cooler For Ryzen 7 2700X – Cooler Master MasterLiquid ML360R ARGB Close-Loop AIO

For PC enthusiasts that want the Ryzen 7 2700x to be liquid-cooled instead of utilizing an air cooler, which can potentially cool the Ryzen 7 2700x processor. The Cooler Master MasterLiquid ML360R ARGB AIO Cooler features a truly impressive cooling capacity when compared to various air-coolers.

The MasterLiquid ML360R AIO cooler utilizes a 360 mm radiator, which means that PC builders can install up to three 120 mm fans.

This AIO cooler utilizes Cooler Master’s Dual Dissipation technology, which is powered by a dual-chamber pump that can enhance performance and durability. This technology has hot water, which is heated by the CPU, and then the cooled water is output through the other side of the pump.

This AIO cooler features RGB lighting on both the Pump and on each of the fans to allow for easy customization for the inside of your PC case.

The large radiator and fantastic pump allow this AIO cooler to cool even an overclocked Ryzen 7 2700x with ease easily.
