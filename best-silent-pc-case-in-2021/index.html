Which fan to choose for a quiet PC?

To determine which fan is the most suitable and the most discreet, you have to look at its noise ratio in decibels (dB) divided by rotation speed. However, this is sometimes not enough. In the best case, it is necessary to know a unit weighted at the human frequency: the dB (A), more accurate for our ears.

In addition, knowing the speed and flow of PC case cooling capacity allows you to know which fan is most likely to be efficient. However, the faster the fan turns, the more noise it generates. Therefore, the best efficient and discreet has a low rotation speed with a high flow rate. Remember, the purpose of a fan is to cool components. There are three different fan sizes:

• Small (60mm – 92mm): used on processors and graphics cards

• 120 mm standard is the most common among best silence PC cases, especially in the mid-range.

• The largest (140 mm – 200 mm) where 140 mm are present on significant cases or high-end cases. They are handy for a front fan.
