Streaming on Twitch or YouTube does not always require the best equipment to do the job but if you want the best experience providing entertainment, consider investing in some high-quality products. Purchasing the equipment does require a little research to know the reliability and quality of the products you have in mind. It does not always hurt to go a little further to purchase a headset that has extra features on it that others do not. If you have a full set up, one of the most important pieces of equipment you need is a headset to hear the game or music with the best sound quality along with the ability to communicate to the streams chat with a high-quality microphone. Two more key aspects in purchasing a headset are knowing that it is comfortable and has the fitting that you desire, as well as knowing if you would like the headset to be wireless or wired. Always know what you are looking for and pay attention to detail so you can find the headset to give you better gaming and streaming experience. Don’t forget to compare prices between the headsets as some headsets are quite expensive.

Sennheiser Game ONE – Wired

The Sennheiser Game ONE is a wired headset that provides some of the best gaming audio there is to experience. It has a rating of 4 stars on Amazon. The headset comes with very comforting XXL velvet plush pads to allow gaming for hours. Just like the Arctis 7, the Game ONE provides clear, noise-canceling conversations with your chat and friends. The microphone boom also includes a mute feature as you lift it. 

There are volume controls included on the right earpiece as well. The Sennheiser Game ONE is compatible with many platforms that include; PC, MACs, any console, and mobile devices that are equipped with a 3.5 mm audio jack input.

CORSAIR Void PRO RGB – Wireless

The CORSAIR Void PRO RGB is a wireless headset that has a very different design than any other headset that comes with a stand that includes a charging port. The CORSAIR Void PRO RGB is rated at 4 stars on Amazon. The headset is purchasable in two colors, black or white, whichever you prefer. The headset is equipped with LED lights; red to let you know when the headset is on low battery, amber to show that the charge is 20% or higher, as green shows that the headset is fully charged as that provides up to 16 hours of battery life. 

The Void PRO is equipped with a microfiber mesh fabric over memory foam comfort for your hour-long streams. The headset comes with an amazing feature that allows you to travel 40 meters before the headset is finally out of range. The CORSAIR Void PRO RGB is compatible with PC with a USB port, Windows 10, Windows 8, and Windows 7.
