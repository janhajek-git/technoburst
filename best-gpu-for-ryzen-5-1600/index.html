Introducing the Ryzen 5 1600, a mid-range CPU that brings you 6 Cores and 12 threads, which is hard to beat at that price point. If you already own or are planning to build a PC with a Ryzen 5 1600 in it, you should also consider getting a graphics card that matches its performance. However, there are a lot of things to consider when buying a graphics card, so you need to make sure that you buy the right GPU to partner with your Ryzen 5 1600.

Things You Need To Consider:

Screen Resolution

If you have a higher resolution monitor, we recommend you to get a much powerful GPU that can handle the workload. But If you only play at 1080p resolution, a decent card that can provide exactly the power you need is the great choice for you.

Refresh Rate

If you have a monitor with a refresh rate of 60hz, and you don’t have any plan to upgrade soon. We recommend you to get a graphics card that is capable of throwing exactly or not more than 60fps. You don’t need to spend more on getting a graphics card that pushers 100+ fps or more since your monitor is only capable of displaying 60fps. However, if you are future-proofing your build and planning to get a higher refresh rate monitor soon, you better get a much more powerful GPU that can provide more FPS.

FreeSync And G-Sync

You can ignore this if your monitor doesn’t feature any Adaptive-Sync at all, but if you already own or are planning to buy a monitor with G-Sync or FreeSync feature, you need to make sure that your monitor and graphics card matches the same features. G-Sync monitors only work with Nvidia, so if you have a G-Sync monitor or planning to buy soon, you need to get an Nvidia graphics card. On the other side, if you already have a FreeSync monitor or planning to buy soon, AMD graphics cards are the best ones for you. These Adaptive-Sync technologies are mainly for resolving screen tearing issues for a much pleasing visual performance.

The Games You Play

It also depends on what games you’re playing, some games are more graphically demanding, so you might wanna get a much powerful GPU in order to handle it. But if you only play less-graphics intensive games, you better get a lower-end GPU.

Hopefully, you now have an idea of what type of graphics card you need. We’ve sorted our list of graphics cards from the most affordable to the most powerful one that you can partner with your Ryzen 5 1600 CPU. So, let’s go and check them out!

Best Mid-Range GPU For Ryzen 5 1600

I recommend you to get these mid-range graphics cards if you only play a 1080p low refresh rate or not graphically demanding games. There’s no point in getting such a powerful graphics card if your current hardware can’t handle the power it’s throwing. The best thing about getting a mid-range graphics card is that you can keep your rig cost down and invest in other components needed on your gaming build. However, if you are future-proofing your build, and planning to upgrade the other components soon, you might wanna consider getting a graphics card that matches them in order to avoid bottlenecking.

Here are the mid-range graphics cards that we recommend to partner with your Ryzen 5 1600.

ASUS GeForce GTX 1060

I want to introduce the ASUS GeForce GTX 1060. It is a mid-range graphics card perfect for 1080p gaming and for games that aren’t graphically demanding. We’ve seen a lot of positive reviews about the performance of the ASUS GeForce GTX 1060. This graphics card has a base clock of 1594 Mhz and can boost up to 1809 Mhz, which is enough to run most games smoothly.

It also features G-Sync Technology, which mainly aims for resolving screen tearing issues along with an amazing dual fan setup that provides 105% more air pressure and 3x quieter than the reference graphics card like the founder edition GTX 1060. This graphics card will surely provide the performance you need in gaming, and it is one of the best graphics cards that we recommend to partner with your Ryzen 5 1600.

We recommend this ASUS GeForce GTX 1060 if you have a G-Sync monitor, but if you have a FreeSync monitor, you must check out the next card in our list, which is the AMD Radeon RX 570 graphics card.

We’ve listed the best high-end graphics card with a great price-to-performance ratio that you can partner with your Ryzen 5 1600. So you can achieve better gaming performance without breaking the bank.
