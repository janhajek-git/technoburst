The ultimate flagship of the new Zen 3 Ryzen 5000 Series of CPUs – and currently the most powerful consumer-grade processor available – is the Ryzen 9 5950X. With a massive 16 cores, 32 threads, and 64 MB of L3 Cach, all fit into just two Core Complexes (CCX), this CPU has now also proven that performs on par with its specifications; and completely dominates its competition in TechSpot’s benchmarks.

In order to harness the full potential this CPU is capable of, be it for workstation or for gaming applications, it will need RAM that is reliable, has a solid thermal solution, and, above all, has the right frequency/latency combination (which together calculate the absolute latency, in nanoseconds).

To provide you options for the perfect memory kit for your setup, we have compiled a list of RAM models suitable to enhance the Ryzen 9 5950X’s processing power – and we have done so based on price, quality, and availability.

Note: All prices are for dual channel kits of 16 GB (2x 8 GB) storage.

Best Gaming RAM for Ryzen 9 5950X – TEAMGROUP T-Force Dark Pro

Though somewhat overkill for a new gaming build, the R9 5950X is still the most powerful consumer CPU available in the market, even for gaming. A RAM kit with a 3,200 MHz frequency and a Column Address Strobe (CAS) latency of 14 cycles (8.75 nanoseconds absolute latency) will suffice for achieving the maximum gaming performance that this CPU can achieve.

The TeamGroup T-Force Dark Pro features XMP 2.0 compatibility, allowing you to save overclocking profiles (like a 3,600 MHz at CL 15) and access them in one step. Additionally, with its aluminum heat spreader and five-layer heatsink, you won’t have to worry about the RAM overheating – though a RAM fan-kit like the Corsair Dominator Platinum can always be used to provide additional cooling, together with an RGB lighting display which the T-Force Dark Pro lacks.

In terms of hardware components that optimize performance, the T-Force Dark Pro has both a JEDEC RC 2.0 custom circuit, which separates power and signal layers to avoid any interference, as well as a Samsung B-Die, which is the most optimal die for Ryzen CPUs. It has excellent CL timings of 14-14-14-31, which provide it with the ability to reach data transfer speeds of up to 24.6 GB/s.

The T-Force Dark Pro can currently be found at an incredibly low price of only $129.99, so do be sure to check it out if you’re looking for a RAM kit to accompany the R9 5950X for a new gaming rig.

Best Low-Profile RAM for Ryzen 9 5950X – Corsair Vengeance LPX

Going a step above the 8.75 ns latency of the 3,200 MHz, CL 14 RAM kit, we have the Corsair Vengeance LPX: which features a frequency of 3,600 MHz, at a CAS latency of only 14 cycles, allowing it to achieve an absolute latency of 7.78 ns.

The Corsair Vengeance LPX, with its pure aluminum heat spread and custom-built PCB, allows for high-level overclocking that can be used for gaming and workstation applications alike. Like the aforementioned T-Force Dark Pro, it also has XMP 2.0 support, together with a low-profile design, that makes it compatible with all PC form factors, and won’t get in the way of a high-end air cooler like the NH-D15. The Vengeance LPX also has individually screened Integrated Circuits (IC), which translates to reliability and increased durability.

Because this kit is devoid of any RGB displays or additional external fluff, it’s price is low enough that it allows for several of these units to be purchased, for larger amounts of memory storage.

In fact, for only $179.99, one can attain 16 GB of dual-channel Corsair Vengeance LPX 3,600 MHz CL 14 RAM to pair with the Ryzen 9 5950X.

Best High-Frequency RAM for Ryzen 9 5950X – G.Skill Trident Z Royal

Any frequencies above the 3,000 MHz mark is where we begin seeing diminishing returns in terms of performance, as there is a limit to how low absolute latency can go for the current generation, DDR4, memory units. Still, several workstation applications do need high-frequency RAM in order to better perform their associated tasks. If high-speed RAM is what you seek, then G.Skill’s Trident Z Royal 4,800 MHz CL 18 RAM is the way to go.

With an incredible absolute latency of 7.5 nanoseconds, the G.Skill Trident Z can run without the need for overclocking, though, of course, it can be overclocked to any speed that your motherboard can support. G.Skill’s Royal Series features an all-aluminum, CNC-cut, heat spreader (in either silver or gold color), hand-screened memory ICs, a custom 10-layer PCB, XMP 2.0 support, and a limited lifetime warranty. Its exterior aesthetics feature a unique jewel design, with a crystalline light-bar and an eight-zone controllable RGB display.

Of course, having such a high baseline frequency means that this RAM is definitely not cheap. Still, not having to worry about overclocking, as well as knowing that the RAM kit will have the reliability associated with G.Skill’s brand, makes its $509.00 price tag justifiable.

Best Overall Performance RAM for Ryzen 9 5950X – G.Skill Trident Z Neo

Reaching a speed faster, even than that of a 4,800 MHz, CL 18 RAM module, we have the G.Skill Trident Z Neo’s 3,800 MHz CL 14 model. This combination of frequency and latency translates to an insane 7.37 nanosecond absolute latency, which is close to the best speed that baseline DDR4 RAM can inherently achieve.

To provide context to this claim, we can simply compare this model with a 5,000 MHz CL 18 RAM module, which offers a 7.20 ns latency (a difference of 0.17 ns), yet costs over $1,000 for two ranks of 8 GB: Almost five-times the cost of this 16 GB kit.

The Trident Z Neo offers everything a Ryzen 9 5950X processor needs, in order to achieve high performance. A Samsung B-Die, an aluminum heat spreader, a 10-layer PCB, hand-screened ICs, and XMP 2.0 compatibility. It also features a stylish design, with a dual-tone, brushed aluminum, and powder-coated exterior, as well as an eight-zone RGB lighting display.

The most impressive part about this RAM kit is its price. For only $219.92 you can attain memory that can be overclocked to the maximum speeds your motherboard can handle, at a low latency level and a reliable thermal solution. The ultimate RAM for the Ryzen 9 5950X.
