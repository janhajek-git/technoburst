MSI GS66 Stealth Lightweight RTX 3080 laptop:

The MSI GS66 Stealth laptop has an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080 graphics accelerator with 16 GB of DDR4-3200 RAM and 16 GB of GDDR6 video memory, which can be independently expanded to 64 GB.

MSI GS66 Stealth Lightweight RTX 3080 laptop weighs 2.1 kg, which is a bit by the standards of gaming models. Its pixel density is 188 PPI, which is enough to avoid seeing ladders in small print. The crystalline effect is also invisible. The brightness of 300 nits is enough for comfortable gaming in a lit room, and the matte finish eliminates reflections and glare under direct light. It is a perfect combination because it comes with a set of ports, a strict design, an excellent display, a valuable battery, a high-quality touchpad, and two slots for SSD. 

The GS66 Stealth’s design goes against gaming patterns. Such rigor will appeal to those who already have their eyes twitching from the luridness of most gaming laptops. The 15.6-inch screen is made using IPS technology, has a resolution of 2560×1440 and a refresh rate of 240 Hz. 

The MSI GS66 Stealth Lightweight laptop received three USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-A ports, HDMI 2.0b, two USB Type C, and 2.5-Gigabit Ethernet. The screen is easy to lift with one hand; the top bezel houses the webcam and faces scanning system. The latter works in the infrared range and recognizes the user even in complete darkness.

Outcomes

MSI GS66 Stealth 10UH comes in a top-end filling compact body that grabs the attention of all gamers.
