The Nvidia RTX 2060 is a mid-range graphics card that will make your desired gaming performance come true. It almost doubled the performance of its predecessor GTX 1060. So, if you already have one, or you are planning to build a PC with RTX 2060 in it, you might wanna consider having a monitor that coordinates its capacity to boost your visual gaming execution.

Today, we will show you the list of the best monitors to pair with the RTX 2060 graphics card. With this graphics card, we would recommend you to go with either a 1080p or 1440p monitor since it is the sweetest spot for gaming. Also, there are many things you need to consider when buying a monitor.

We also have a list of the best monitors to pair with the RTX 2070 graphics card if you want to check it out.

Things You Might Wanna Consider:

Resolution & Refresh Rate

If you are a competitive gamer, I would recommend you to go with the 1080p monitor. 1080p monitors are still the standard resolution for most serious gamers. Pro players tend to play at 1080p gaming because it is easy to achieve higher FPS, which results in much smoother gameplay. On the other side, if you want a much sharper image, 1440p resolution is the best for you. But remember that it will be harder to achieve higher FPS since 1440p gaming requires high computational power.

If you decided to get a 1080p monitor, I would recommend you to go with a 165hz refresh rate, this will give you enough space because you will be expecting that your graphics card will throw more FPS on it. But for the 1440 monitor, a refresh rate that matches the FPS you’re getting is a good choice for you. Choosing a monitor is completely up to you, it will always vary depending on your preferences whether you prefer visuals or performance. Here are the recommended resolution and refresh rate pairings:

  • 1920x1080p at 165Hz
  • 2560x1440p at 144Hz
  • 2560×1080(ultra wide) at 75Hz

G-Sync Compatibility

You might also consider getting a monitor with G-Sync compatibility, If you already have the RTX 2060 graphics card, so you can take advantage of Nvidia’s adaptive sync technology, which mainly aims for resolving display stutter and screen tearing issues to achieve a much pleasing gaming experience.

Best 1440p Monitor For RTX 2060 – Acer Predator XB271HU

1440p Monitors are getting popular throughout the years. This type of monitor can provide you both fast performance and sharper images. 1440p monitors aren’t that fast as 1080p monitors, but they are 30-40 percent sharper, and almost doubled the performance of the 4k monitors. We highly recommend you to get a 1440p monitor if you want a balanced choice between sharp images and fast performance. We ended up picking the Acer Predator XB271HU since it is the best 1440p monitor that we’ve found that has the right features and capabilities we need for the RTX 2060 graphics card.

The Acer Predator XB271HU is a 27-inch screen size gaming monitor. It also features G-Sync technology that fixes screen tearing issues and an IPS panel, which provides more colors and better viewing angles for a much pleasing gameplay performance.

The Acer Predator XB271HU comes with a 144hz refresh rate, and a 4ms response time, which is pretty good enough for achieving decent performance. I would highly recommend this monitor if you only play slow-paced games like RPG. Although we can’t keep away from accepting the fact that the RTX 2060 graphics card isn’t that powerful enough to run AAA games consistently above 60fps, but there are still games out there that are not graphically demanding, so you can still achieve higher frames rate and smooth gameplay performance with the 144hz refresh rate monitor. It might be a bit pricey to get the 144hz monitor, but it sounds to be a good investment for future-proofing your gaming build like if you plan to upgrade the graphics card in the future or perhaps want to run dual RTX 2060.

In terms of the design, you can notice with its appearance that it is built for gaming. It comes with an amazing gamer look and an incredibly sturdy two red metal legs that look like a landing gear of a spaceship. Also, it features amazing ergonomics, which allows you to tilt, swivel, and adjust the height so it will be easy for you to achieve your desired viewing angle. Overall, the Acer Predator XB271HU could be the best 1440 monitor that we recommend to pair with your RTX 2060 graphics card.

Best UltraWide Monitor For RTX 2060 – LG 34UM68-P

Of course, we have an entry for the ultra-wide monitor. UltraWide monitors have gotten amazingly well known in recent years. We are also big fans of ultra-widescreen monitors since this type of monitor is great not just for gaming but also for multitasking. For individuals who want to achieve an immersive gaming experience or want to increase productivity, we highly recommend you to get an UltraWide monitor. 

We ended up picking the LG 34UM68-P, since it is the best ultra-wide monitor we’ve found, and could be a perfect pair for the RTX 2060 graphics card. It is a 34-inch monitor that features an IPS panel, which is ideal for ultrawide monitors. It also comes with a response time of 5ms, ideal if you only play slow-paced games like RPG. With its 75Hz refresh rate and FreeSync technology, this monitor helps you keep things smooth as possible. Overall, we highly recommend you the LG 34UM68-P if you really prefer UltraWide screen monitors. With its very affordable price, which is less than $400. This monitor fits well into the spending plan for a mid-range gaming system.
