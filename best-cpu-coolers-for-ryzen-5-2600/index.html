One of the downsides to the Corsair A500 Dual-Fan CPU cooler is the price and the overall size, and it is quite massive compared to other CPU coolers and the noise level produced by the dual fans that are installed.

The biggest issue for CPU coolers is ensuring that they are compatible with the RAM sticks that you may have bought for the system already. One unique feature to the A500 is the slide and lock fan mounting system, which easily allows for the moving and much more comprehensive support for RAM height.

Cooler Master MasterLiquid Lite 240 CPU Liquid Cooler

Some PC users prefer using liquid cooling instead of air coolers. For those users, we highly recommend you the MasterLiquid Lite 240. This AIO cooler offers a fantastic performance to noise generation ratio, as it uses a combination of Cooler Master exclusive technology and an Air Balance fan design.

The cooling capacity of this fantastic AIO cooler is a perfect choice for the PC users that want to overclock the Ryzen 5 2600 processor, another feature that makes this AIO cooler perfect for overclockers is the quality precision copper base and the dual-chamber pump.

To cool the low resistance 240 mm radiator, this AIO cooler features two Masterfan Air Balance 120.

However, the MasterLiquid Lite 240 has two main disadvantages compared to the standard CPU air coolers, which are the overall size and the tubing length. The MasterLiquid Lite 240 AIO cooler is significantly larger than even the Corsair A500 Air cooler; this is due to the included 240 mm radiator, which will require a case that can support the larger radiator. The second downside to the MasterLiquid Lite 240, which is prevalent in all AIO coolers, is the length of the tubes that connect the pump to the radiator.

The price of this is just a bit more than the MasterAir G100M CPU cooler, costing just $54.99. For only $14 more than the MasterAir G100M, users can get this fantastic cooler, although your case will have to offer support for the larger cooler.

Overall, this MasterLiquid Lite 240 is one of our top recommendation CPU Coolers to pair with the Ryzen 5 2600, especially if you are planning to perform an overclocking.
