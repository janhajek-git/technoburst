Intel Core i9-9900K processor has been the best processor for gaming for years, AMD is slowly catching up and may have surpassed the Intel Core i9-9900K processor, but many currently use the Intel Core i9-9900K in their gaming setups. The Intel Core i9-9900K features eight cores and a total of sixteen threads with a maximum turbo frequency of 5.00 GHz. This processor launched back in 2018, which has a maximum TDP of 95-watts.

For the Intel Core i9-9900K having a fast RAM speed and lowest CAS latency possible, these specifications are highly important for any Gaming or video editing computer.

Pairing the Intel Core i9-9900K with the correct RAM is important, some softwares and games require higher available RAM or need faster RAM speeds.

This article focuses on four different RAM kits which are perfect for a variety of different scenarios ranging from value RAM kits to even the fanciest RAM kit. This wide variety of different RAM kits allow for PC builders to buy the RAM kit that fits best with their perfect PC system.
