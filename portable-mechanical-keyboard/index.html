If you consider it’s time to replace your old keyboard, getting a portable mechanical keyboard instead of a normal one might be a better idea. Compared to normal keyboards, portable mechanical keyboards are easier to carry around because of their compact size. These keyboards don’t feature a number pad, and they are usually used for gaming or work thanks to their durability and longer lifespan. Despite the fact that the number pad is missing, the layout of a portable mechanical keyboard is similar to a normal keyboard.

Usually, portable mechanical keyboards cost more than full-size keyboards. However, they come with many high-tech features. Because of its size, it is easier to organize a desk, and it’s very comfortable to use. Normally, they are equipped with mechanical switches, and you can also remove and replace the keycaps. This type of keyboard is especially suitable for people who spend many hours a day in front of a computer. Its space-friendly feature provides mobility, and it’s easy to carry it in a laptop bag or a backpack.

If you want to find out more, here is a list of the best portable mechanical keyboards. This list contains reviews and information for each keyboard to provide more insight.
