How Do I Pick a Good CPU Cooler for i9-11900k?

Having decided on the type of equipment needed to cool your system fully, you should pay attention to the following aspects. If you do not know how to choose a cooler for 11th gen processor, please note that we only disclose the key parameters, but we cannot give any recommendations, not knowing what kind of hardware you have. So:

Radiator: Today, aluminum models are found less and less; copper is more often used. The second material has better thermal conductivity and guarantees more efficient heat dissipation.

The number of heat pipes: These elements contain a low-boiling liquid, which evaporates and condenses at the other end, which will allow removing unnecessary heat from the source and transferring it to the radiator. Inexpensive active coolers, there are up to 6 such elements, whereas in premium ones, there are more than six.

Mounting method: To install passive and active coolers, you can use latches, double-sided mount types, bolts, and silicone mounts. The latter option is prioritized due to vibration absorption.

Fan quality: The most common 120mm fans can have different bearings: sliding, rolling, and fluid dynamics. The last bearings are filled with a special fluid that guarantees good mobility during the fan’s rotation.

Turnovers: The lower the minimum – the wider the adjustment range, the higher – the more effective. In this case, the diameter is closely related to the size of the fan. It is considered the norm from 1000 to 2500 rpm.

Therefore, it is worth paying attention to any modernization of the cooling system; otherwise, the consequences can range from excessive spending of money to overheating the Northbridge or processor power supplies. However, competently assembled cooling will give both silence and a constant temperature of the processor.
