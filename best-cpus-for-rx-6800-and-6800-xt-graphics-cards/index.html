It’s configured as an upgrade over an RTX GeForce Ti 2080, which means it’ll compete with the RTX GeForce 3070. As per AMD, you can get more than 90 frames per second at 1440p.

Radeon Rx Amd 6800 Xt & Rx Radeon 6800 Reference Models

AMD’s relationship brands, including Gigabyte, MSI, Asus, ASRock, XFX, Sapphire, & Powercolor, are willing to design the respective Radeon GPU-powered graphics cards and those with AIO fluid coolers. 

However, several of the Graphic cards on the market based on AMD’s sample layout. Among the most widely available products in India, Sapphire would distribute cards similar to reference units that we were evaluating now.

AMD does not sell its very own cards, as Nvidia is doing with Founders Variant versions, but a significant effort put into the development. The RX Radeon 6800 XT & RX 6800 comparison cards were heavy & well built.  

Each has evaporation chamber chillers as well as a die-cast aluminium cover along with three custom-designed axisymmetric fans. When the graphics cards are not moving, the fan base will completely turn off.

All of this improves acoustic & cooling performance, and all these graphics cards should be smoother than past ones. Nonetheless, this development is said to enable some extent of overclocking, which will keep fans happy. 

AMD claims that graphite patches used to communicate between the Graphics card as well as the cooler. It is used instead of thermal material that can dry out through time. Thermal pads also used to improve heat capacitance between cooler, GDDR6 storage, & power circuitry.

In contrast to Nvidia’s latest shrunken specification, which includes an adapter, AMD has been using regular 8-pin PCIe energy connectors. Both RX Radeon XT 6800 & the RX Radeon-6800 have two 8-pin connections, mainly on the back that face upwards.

The RX Radeon XT 6800 reference design takes up 2.5 spaces, whereas the RX-6800 is slightly slimmer. Both of these are regular 10.5-inch lengths. AMD was using its trademark reddish colour as a decoration from around the exposed heatsink vent on the bottom, & each card features a red-coloured Radeon logo.

Both graphic cards include 1 HDMI 2.1 slot, 2 DisplayPort 1.4 slots, and an unusual Type-C USB port. AMD claims that this enables single-wire connectivity to head-mounted screens because it can provide a digital signal and up to 27W of energy. 

If you have a triple-monitor system, you may need some adapters. The use of USB explores the path for potential displays that can be daisy-chained & include a USB hub and other compatible peripheral devices. But that is an attractive option given that the VirtualLink norm is essentially dead, & Nvidia eliminated this feature from the Founders Version cards just after 1 generation.
